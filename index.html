    About Chacha's Body-Logic Scrubs

    Welcome to Chacha's Body-Logic Scrubs! We are passionate about creating natural, high-quality scrubs that leave your skin feeling soft, refreshed, and glowing.OUR BODY SCRUB IS OF ORIGIN, NOT REPACKAGED AND NOT PRO MIXED. WE HAVE CAREFULLY SELECTED OUR INGREDIENTS TO CREATE THE VARIETY OF BEAUTIFUL FLAVORS THAT WE STOCK

    Our mission is to provide luxurious skincare products that are affordable, natural, and crafted with love. Every product is carefully made to ensure your skin receives the best care possible. And also to see adults, adolescents and the generation to come, do away with all harmful beauty/skin products that put ones health at great risk.

    Meet the Founder

    Ms Chansa Chibesa

    ChaCha’s organics is a Zambian brand of natural self-care products that has been on the market since 2020. Due to its quality and uniqueness and careful selection of the finest natural ingredients in its products, in just a few months, ChaCha’s organics has been getting recognition on social media and Lusaka areas. Starting with only 10 jars of our natural body scrub, we can proudly say that we are growing into an established brand of natural products with both social media one on one customer interaction.
